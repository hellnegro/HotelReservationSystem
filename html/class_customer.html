Detailed Description

Represents a customer in the hotel management system.

The Customer class inherits from the Human class and adds specific attributes for managing customer information, such as phone number and email. It provides constructors for creating customer objects with various parameters, getter and setter methods for accessing and modifying customer attributes, and a method to retrieve customer's full name.
